The Superpower of Stories

We exist to empower every global Indian child with this superpower — so they grow up rooted in their culture, alive with imagination, and ready to shape the world.

We show up — live, interactive, and present — to give every child the skills, confidence, and cultural pride to own their voice.

Small groups, real teachers, real conversations. Every session is designed so children actively participate — not just listen.

Life Skills

Communication, critical thinking, empathy, and confidence — woven into every story we tell and every discussion we lead.

🇮🇳

Cultural Pride

Rooted in Indian heritage, enriched by stories from around the world — so every child carries their culture with pride.
