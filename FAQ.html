Everything you need to know about joining the One Katha at a Time community.

What age groups are the storytelling courses designed for? ▼
Our core programs are designed for children aged 6 to 14, with specific groups for younger learners (6-9) and teenagers (10-14) to ensure age-appropriate narratives and complexity.
Is there a focus on Indian culture in every session? ▼
Yes! While we explore global mythologies and universal human truths, we anchor every session in Indian cultural heritage, including epics, folklore, and historical narratives.
How large are the live interactive groups? ▼
To maintain a high level of personalized mentorship, each live session is limited to a maximum of 12 storytellers. This ensures every child has the space and confidence to find their voice.
Can we join from any timezone? ▼
Absolutely! We have learners connecting from over 15 countries. We schedule our live sessions to accommodate multiple timezones, particularly for the Global Indian Diaspora.
